Explore the Nature

Mountain winters mesmerize each soul for its fairytale-like views that can be found only when one has the courage to head outdoors. Every year, the drizzle of snow beautifies every trail, path, and town of the mountainous region. With videos and pictures circulating year after year, mountain aficionados at some point in their life wish to undertake a snow trek.

Make your trek adventerous

Trekking in India promises you an undisturbed experience in the lap of nature, where you can explore a great variety of flora and fauna. At its lowest elevations, the Valley of Flowers has jungles of birch, firs and maple, which at higher elevations are replaced by herbs, shrubs and grass. Animals like brown bears, snow leopards, tahrs, musk deer, ghorals, red foxes and black bears live in the mountains in plenty. Come join us to start your adventerous journey.
