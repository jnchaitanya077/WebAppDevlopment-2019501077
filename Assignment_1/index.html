My Journey in MSIT

MSIT's method of "learning by doing" requires learning in a project-centric manner. When applying ideas this helps us to solve problems easily. The course is divided into manageable modules, each of which is presented as a workable project. We work in teams with an atmosphere comparable to corporate. When working on the project, we can understand the definition and submit specific tasks (deliverables) within the deadline defined by their mentors. We get the required guidance and encouragement from the mentors at every level, who have a large knowledge base that improves our knowledge.
